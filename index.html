Theodore Bland

A Doctor, a Farmer, a soilder and a politition, Theodore Bland was a founding father. He served in the Continital army under Washington. During the war he also served in the Virginia House of Burgeusses and the Cotenental Congress. He served in the national congress, and finally in the House of Representatives, where he died in office.

In all he served as a:

  • Solder
  • Representative, House of Burgesses
  • Congressman
